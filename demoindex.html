Refurwish Shield Plan Terms & Conditions

Extended Warranty Plan (12 Months Extended)

  • Covers functional defects arising after the manufacturer's warranty expires, for an additional 12 months.
  • Includes repair or replacement for mechanical/electrical breakdowns.
  • Does not cover physical damage, liquid damage, battery, accessories, or software issues.
  • Repair will be attempted first; if irreparable, a replacement (same model/specifications) or a depreciated refund will be provided.

Screen Damage Protection Plan (12 Months)

  • Covers accidental physical damage to the device screen (front display glass) for 12 months from purchase.
  • One-time repair/replacement for screen damage during the plan period.
  • Does not cover liquid damage, total loss, or damage affecting other parts of the device beyond the screen.
  • Deductible may apply as per policy.

Total Damage Protection Plan (12 Months)

  • Comprehensive coverage for accidental physical damage and liquid damage for 12 months.
  • Includes coverage for drops, spills, and other accidental damage.
  • One-time repair/replacement for device damage during the plan period.
  • Does not cover theft, loss, cosmetic damage, or manufacturer defects.
  • Deductible may apply as per policy.

Combo Plan: Screen Damage Protection + Extended Warranty (24 Months Total)

  • **Screen Damage Protection:** Covers accidental screen damage for the first 12 months.
  • **Extended Warranty:** Covers functional defects for 12 months following the manufacturer's warranty (total 24 months coverage for functional defects).
  • Combines benefits of both plans. Terms specific to each component (Screen Damage / Extended Warranty) apply as outlined above for their respective periods.
  • Deductibles and exclusions specific to each component apply.

Combo Plan: Total Damage Protection + Extended Warranty (24 Months Total)

  • **Total Damage Protection:** Covers accidental physical and liquid damage for the first 12 months.
  • **Extended Warranty:** Covers functional defects for 12 months following the manufacturer's warranty (total 24 months coverage for functional defects).
  • Combines benefits of both plans. Terms specific to each component (Total Damage / Extended Warranty) apply as outlined above for their respective periods.
  • Deductibles and exclusions specific to each component apply.

General Terms Applicable to All Plans:

  • Device must be in perfect working condition at the time of plan purchase.
  • Claims require original purchase invoice, plan certificate, and government-issued ID.
  • Repair/replacement decisions are at Refurwish's sole discretion.
  • Coverage limits and specific exclusions apply as detailed in the full policy document.
  • The plan is non-transferable and applicable only to the device with the registered IMEI.
